WTF is going on here?

What would it take to actually take bitcoin to the moon?
As in land some private keys on the moon.

Why?

Imagine bitcoin's largest PR stunt, in history. Pulling off something so magnificent, hilarious, and epic that would garner world-wide attention of a digital meme being brought into life.

But it would be the gift that keeps on giving.
Because once the address is on the moon, the associated address could become a popular burn address. Funds will trickle into the wallet, and overtime, the price of bitcoin will rise and the cost of space travel will decrease. It will create a race to the moon and grab the private keys.
